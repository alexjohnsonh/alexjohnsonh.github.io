Colour Theory

Colour theory is the study of how colours interact with each other and how they can be used to create certain visual effects. It involves understanding the relationships between colours, such as complementary colours, analogous colours, and triadic colours.

Colour theory also explores the psychological and emotional impact of colours on human perception. Different colours can evoke specific emotions and moods, making colour selection an important aspect of design and art.

By understanding colour theory, designers and artists can make informed decisions about colour schemes, create harmonious colour palettes, and effectively communicate their intended message through the use of colour.

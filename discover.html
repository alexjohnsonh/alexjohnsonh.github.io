Colour

‘Colour’ is an animated piece that explores our relationship with the chromatic world around us. Born from my lifelong fascination with colour – from organising apps to curating outfits – this work challenges viewers to see beyond the functional. Through research, I found many only engage with colour when necessary, missing the rich visual language that surrounds us daily. This animation invites audiences to discover the hidden beauty in every shade, transforming the ordinary into the extraordinary. It’s an invitation to pause and appreciate how colour shapes our environments, influences our emotions, and adds depth to our everyday experiences.
